Daily dose of weather and jokes

Daily dose of weather and jokes offers you daily forecasts of six different Finnish and a joke. Cities where you can check the forecast: Oulu, Helsinki, Tampere, Lahti, Turku and Rovaniemi. You can choose the joke from three different categories: blonde, animal or just a random joke. This website is part of the "Web-tekniikat" course. API:s used on this site: weatherapi.com   Jokes API

Choose city and a category. Then press the buttons to print or erase information
